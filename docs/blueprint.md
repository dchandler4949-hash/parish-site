# **App Name**: PolicyPath

## Core Features:

- Static Page Management: Ability to create, update, and manage all informational pages like Home, About David, Manifesto, Education, Policing, Environment, Housing, Roads, Transport, Poverty, Council Tax & Business Rates, Social Care, Health, Democracy, Accessibility, Cookies Page, Disclaimer, Privacy Page, Survey Page, and Evidence & Resources Page.
- Content Display: Render all static page content in a clean, readable format, supporting text, images, and links.
- Navigation System: A clear, intuitive navigation menu to access all primary and secondary pages of the website.
- Contact Form: A basic contact form for users to send inquiries, capturing necessary contact information.
- GDPR Compliance Features: Include explicit consent mechanisms for cookies and personal data processing as required by GDPR.
- Footer Content: Ensure a consistent footer across all pages displaying the legal imprint, copyright, and links to the Cookies, Disclaimer, and Privacy Pages, designed to comply with UK electoral laws.
- Accessibility Features: Implement features to promote social inclusion, such as options for high contrast, larger text, keyboard navigation, and screen reader compatibility to address visual and disability needs.
- User Surveys: Enable users to participate in interactive surveys to gather public opinion and feedback on various policy topics.
- Evidence & Resources Page: Provide a dedicated page to display supporting evidence, external websites, and links related to policy positions and research.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a trustworthy, deep blue (#2989B8), chosen for its professional and calming association, suitable for conveying policy and civic information.
- The background color is a very light, almost white, blue-grey (#ECF3F5), providing a clean and non-distracting canvas that subtly aligns with the primary blue.
- The accent color is a clear, vibrant violet-blue (#847EE6), used sparingly for interactive elements like buttons and links to provide visual highlight and clear call-to-actions.
- Header background: Black (#000000).
- Header text: White (#FFFFFF) for the name 'David Chandler'.
- Footer background: Black (#000000).
- Footer text: White (#FFFFFF).
- Headlines will use 'Alegreya', a humanist serif font, to convey an elegant, intellectual, and authoritative tone suitable for policy discussion. Body text will use 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif, ensuring excellent readability for extended content like manifestos and detailed policy pages.
- Utilize a consistent set of minimal, outline-style icons to visually aid navigation and content sections without distracting from the text.
- A clean, modern layout with ample whitespace to ensure content is the focus and highly readable. Pages should be responsively designed for optimal viewing across various devices, including website, mobile phones (Android and iPhone), and tablets.
- The header will prominently display 'David Chandler' with a red (#FF0000) rectangular box around it.
- A red (#FF0000) rectangular border will enclose the footer content for emphasis.
- Vertical red (#FF0000) lines will frame the main content area on both the left and right sides, creating a distinct visual boundary.
